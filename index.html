About me

My dream job is to be a Front End Web Developer. After graduating high school in 2007, I moved to England where I earned my Bachelor's Degree in Forensic Science in 2015. After working at an engine oil additive company until 2019, I moved back home to Illinois.

My first experience with web development was in 2003, when I learned how to use HTML and CSS in order to customize my Neopets shop. After that, I learned how to edit my MySpace page. I really loved coding at the time, but ended up going into the Forensic Science field and earning my degree in it.

After working for 4 years in a laboratory, I realized it isn't what I wanted to do for the rest of my life. After moving back to Illinois, I took on a couple different positions trying to find myself, and during my free time, rediscovered my love for coding. During lockdown, I spent my free time learning C#, HTML, and CSS. I became determined to learn even more and wanted pursue a career in coding, so I took the opportunity to enroll in Coding Temple.
